Who We Are

✻

Educational Power Corp (EPC), is a not-for profit 501 c(3) corporation committed to providing free education, training, classes and services to the community at large and the underserved.

Julia Hidalgo

CEO / Founder

EPC is managed and operated by Julia Hidalgo a certified Paralegal with a Master Degree in Information and Library Science. She has more than 15 years of experience working with legal document preparation, giving educational advice for high school students and offering a wide range of notary services and process bilingual documents, in English and Spanish. We offer a wide selection of affordable Educational, Notary and Paralegal Services. Our services are available in Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach & Monroe counties. We are not licensed members of the Bar and do not practice law. We cannot and does not give legal advice in any matter. After many years of working with hundreds of people who are coming to the library for help, I realize how much important is to have someone to help and guide you to complete legal documents, general paper work to receive financial aid, application to study abroad and general online applications to receive health insurance, Medicaid, or find a good school for your kids. I want to become your friend advisor in legal and educational matters.
